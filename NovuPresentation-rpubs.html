What are the chances that the effect we are seeing from Pilot 2 is a fluke?

  • The Mann-Whitney U-test was performed on the aggregate-by-age data
  • The p-values are significantly below the p-critical of 0.05
  • the p-value is the probability our results were flukes - the null-hypothesis.

Conclusion

     From what I understand, Novu's goal is to work with the Healthcare Industry to bring their constituents up to a higher level of Health and Wellness. For me, that also implies that we are a Health Risk Mitigator. And it is a constant, that the older one gets the higher the chances of health complications become. In the perspective of Insurance Purveyors, the higher the health risk means higher liability for them.

     Although the conversion rates were lower for Pilot 2, it successfully targeted the older, higher-risk demographics, and got them to respond. It should also be noted that regardless of the seemingly lower performance rate, Pilot 2 hit a higher raw conversion count.

     Personally, I would have liked to have performed some A/B tests on the sites themselves. Ideally, we would be able to identify and implement the positive outcomes of the high success rates of Pilot 1 with the appeal to a higher age demographic and raw numbers of Pilot 2 in a 3rd Pilot Campaign.
